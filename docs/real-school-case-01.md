# Real School Case 01

# 真实学校案例 01

## A student who was always late

## 一个总迟到的学生

---

At first, the problem looked simple.

The student was late almost every morning.

最开始，问题看起来很简单。

这个学生几乎每天迟到。

---

Traditional school management usually responds with:

* warnings
* punishment
* parent calls
* discipline tracking

传统学校管理的第一反应通常是：

* 警告
* 批评
* 家长电话
* 德育记录

---

But after several conversations recorded through GetNotes,
a different pattern slowly appeared.

但在几次通过 GetNotes 留下的谈话之后，

另一个问题慢慢浮现出来。

---

The student was not “lazy”.

The student’s parents were going through serious conflict.

The student had started sleeping after 2AM.

这个学生并不是“懒”。

而是父母长期冲突。

孩子经常凌晨两点之后才能睡觉。

---

The school started changing its support strategy.

Instead of only focusing on punishment, the workflow became:

* emotional support
* sleep monitoring
* communication rhythm adjustment
* family coordination
* academic follow-up

于是学校开始调整支持方式。

重点不再只是处罚。

而变成：

* 情绪支持
* 睡眠观察
* 沟通节奏调整
* 家校协调
* 学业跟进

---

The important part was not AI.

The important part was:

the conversations did not disappear anymore.

真正重要的，不是 AI。

真正重要的是：

这些沟通，不再消失了。

---

Several months later,
the student’s attendance stabilized.

But more importantly:

the school finally had continuity.

几个月后，

学生的出勤恢复稳定。

但更重要的是：

学校第一次真正拥有了连续性。

---

# Final Reflection

# 最后的反思

Most schools do not fail because teachers do not care.

Most schools fail because support work cannot accumulate.

很多学校的问题，

并不是老师不负责。

而是：

支持工作无法积累。
