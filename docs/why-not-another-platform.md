# Why Schools Do Not Need Another Platform

# 为什么学校不需要再多一个平台

---

Schools already have too many systems.

* attendance systems
* grade systems
* communication systems
* online learning systems
* behavior systems

学校已经有太多平台了。

* 考勤系统
* 成绩系统
* 家校系统
* 在线学习系统
* 德育系统

---

But schools are still exhausted.

但学校依然疲惫。

---

The problem is not lack of software.

The problem is fragmentation.

问题不是没有软件。

问题是：

碎片化。

---

Teachers are not afraid of AI.

Teachers are afraid of:

* more forms
* more clicks
* more duplicated work
* more meaningless reporting

老师不是害怕 AI。

老师害怕的是：

* 更多表格
* 更多点击
* 更多重复劳动
* 更多无意义汇报

---

That is why Schooling follows one principle:

Do not interrupt real work.

Schooling 的核心原则只有一个：

不要打断真实工作。

---

The system should grow from existing workflows:

* conversations
* meetings
* follow-ups
* communication
* reflection

系统应该从学校已经存在的工作里长出来：

* 谈话
* 会议
* 跟进
* 沟通
* 复盘

---

AI should not become another burden.

AI should become invisible infrastructure.

AI 不应该变成新的负担。

AI 应该变成：

隐形基础设施。

---

# Final Thought

# 最后的话

The best school technology
is often the technology teachers barely notice.

最好的学校技术，

往往是老师几乎感觉不到它存在的技术。
